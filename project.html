What Makes a Game "Retro"?

To me, a retro game is any classic video game released before the year 2000, during the golden era of 8-bit and 16-bit gaming. These games represent a time when gameplay innovation and creativity flourished despite technological limitations.

Retro games are characterized by their pixel art graphics, memorable chiptune music, and pure gameplay mechanics that focused on fun rather than photorealism. They challenged players with genuine difficulty and rewarded skill and perseverance.

This collection represents my personal favorites that left lasting impressions and continue to inspire modern game design today.
